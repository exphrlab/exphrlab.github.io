iSTEP Study

We are currently recruiting adults aged 65 and older in the Atlanta area to participate in our iSTEP (Individualized Strength Training and Exercise Program) study. This research examines how personalized exercise interventions can support brain health, cognitive function, and physical independence in older adults.

Participants will receive individualized exercise training and have the opportunity to contribute to important research on healthy aging.

Sign Up for iSTEP Study

TRAC Study

The TRAC (Training, Recovery, and Cognitive Health) study investigates the relationship between exercise, recovery, and cognitive function in older adults. We are seeking participants who are interested in learning how different types of physical activity impact brain health and daily functioning.

Sign Up for TRAC Study
