Who We Are

Exercise for Precision Health and Rehabilitation

At ExPHR (Exercise for Precision Health and Rehabilitation), we are dedicated to supporting healthy aging through evidence-based lifestyle interventions.

Our mission is to help midlife and older adults — with and without chronic health conditions — adopt sustainable habits in exercise, nutrition, and sleep that promote long-term brain and body health.

Our research investigates the neural, physiological, and psychological mechanisms that drive behavior change and resilience, with a particular focus on how exercise supports brain function and cognitive health across the aging process. We also design music-based interventions to enhance rehabilitation and motivation among individuals with neurologic or mobility limitations.

Ultimately, our goal is to develop accessible, enjoyable, and lasting physical activity strategies that empower older adults to maintain independence, improve quality of life, and promote optimal aging.

If you are interested in learning more or collaborating with our lab, please contact us.

Research Focus Areas

Healthy Aging

Lifestyle interventions for midlife and older adults with and without chronic conditions.

Brain & Cognitive Health

Neural and physiological mechanisms linking exercise to cognitive function.

Behavior Change

Psychological drivers of sustainable exercise, nutrition, and sleep habits.

Music-Based Rehab

Rhythm and music interventions for neurologic and mobility rehabilitation.
