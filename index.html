About EXPHR Lab

At ExPHR, we are dedicated to supporting healthy aging through evidence-based lifestyle change. Our mission is to help midlife and older adults ‚Äî with and without chronic health conditions ‚Äî adopt sustainable habits in exercise, nutrition, and sleep that protect long-term brain and body health.

Our research investigates the neural and psychological mechanisms that drive behavior change and resilience, with a particular focus on how exercise can support brain function as we age. We also develop music-based interventions to support rehabilitation in individuals with neurologic impairments.

Ultimately, our goal is to create accessible, enjoyable, and lasting physical activity strategies that empower older adults to maintain independence, enhance quality of life, and promote optimal aging.

If you are interested in working with our lab, please contact us.
