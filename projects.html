Projects

ISTEP

Inspiring Seniors Toward Exercise Promotion (ISTEP) is a music-supported, habit-building physical activity program for midlife and older adults. We combine rhythmic auditory stimulation with progressive, enjoyable exercise to improve brain and heart health while building sustainable daily movement.

  • Population: midlife to older adults with/without chronic conditions
  • Approach: music-cued walking and strength, goal-setting, coaching
  • Outcomes: adherence, fitness, cognition, and quality of life

TRAC

TRAC investigates how exercise and daily behaviors support brain–cardio resilience. We track cognitive and affective changes alongside physiological markers to understand mechanisms linking movement, mood, and long-term health.

  • Focus: neural and psychological mechanisms of behavior change
  • Measures: cognition, mood, activity, and cardiorespiratory indices
  • Goal: personalized, enjoyable strategies for sustained activity
